    *Noble Actual*

    Game opens up to a damage Spartan helment with a mountain in the background on a Glassed planet. It then cuts to a new cutscene where we meet SPARTAN-B312. SPARTAN-B312 is introduced to Noble Team. Emile-A239 (Noble 5) and Kat-B320 (Noble 2) Discusses SPARTAN-B312 combat file, which has a lot of parts covered in black ink. Leader of Noble Team: Carter-A259 (Noble 1); gives SPARTAN-B312 the title of Noble 6 (replacing a MIA Spartan). Noble Team hop into a Falcon to investigate an incident of a down relay. Cutscene ends with Noble 1 welcoming Noble 6 to planet Reach

    *Winter Contingency*

    Noble Team is sent to Visegrad, a remote region on Reach. The down relay is Reach's only communication to the rest of the UNSC. The down relay is suspected of foul play by local insurrectionist. As they are walking, they come across the dead bodies of the UNSC troops that were originally sent to fix the relay. They no longer suspect the insurrectionists based on the damage of the dead bodies. They then get ambused by a Covenant force. They fight until they which the relay. They encounter Sangheili Zealots at the relay station. Noble Team force the Sangheili to retreat. Noble Team brings the relay back up and contacts their corresponding higher-up: Colonel Holland, Declaring Winter Contingency.

    *ONI: Sword Base*

    Noble Team is tasked to defend Sword Base after the Covenant begains attacking the base. After taking out a group of Covenant, Noble 2 and 6 are ordered to reactivate a COM array to contact command. After reactivating the COM, they regroup with the rest of Noble Team. Noble Team fights thir way to the top of Sword Base where Noble 5 and 6 takes out several Phantoms and Banshees in order for a pair of Longswords to help. As the Covenant back off from Sword Base, they are shotdown by an Orbital defense platform. Noble Team is then called back into Sword Base by Dr. Halsey. She confront Noble 1 about not pursuing the Sangheili's from at Visegrad. She explains they were an artifact retrieval team from the Covenant and that Visegrad had valuable data, in which Noble 2 had retrieve and gives to Halsey. Noble Team leaves the base as Halsey studies the data.
