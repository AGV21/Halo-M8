    *Noble Actual*

    Game opens up to a damage Spartan helment with a mountain in the background on a Glassed planet. It then cuts to a new cutscene where we meet SPARTAN-B312. SPARTAN-B312 is introduced to Noble Team. Emile-A239 (Noble 5) and Kat-B320 (Noble 2) Discusses SPARTAN-B312 combat file, which has a lot of parts covered in black ink. Leader of Noble Team: Carter-A259 (Noble 1); gives SPARTAN-B312 the title of Noble 6 (replacing a MIA Spartan). Noble Team hop into a Falcon to investigate an incident of a down relay. Cutscene ends with Noble 1 welcoming Noble 6 to planet Reach

    *Winter Contingency*

    Noble Team is sent to Visegrad, a remote region on Reach. The down relay is Reach's only communication to the rest of the UNSC. The down relay is suspected of foul play by local insurrectionist. As they are walking, they come across the dead bodies of the UNSC troops that were originally sent to fix the relay. They no longer suspect the insurrectionists based on the damage of the dead bodies. They then get ambused by a Covenant force. They fight until they which the relay. Noble Team brings the relay back up and contacts their corresponding higher-up: Colonel Holland, Declaring Winter Contingency.

    *ONI: Sword Base*

    Noble Team is tasked to defend Sword Base after the Covenant begains attacking the base. After taking out a group of Covenant, Noble 2 and 6 are ordered to reactivate a COM array to contact command. After reactivating the COM, they regroup with the rest of Noble Team.
