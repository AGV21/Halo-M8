    • *Winter Update*

    Campaign Network Co-op & Mission Replay

    Added ability to play with up to three friends in campaign

    Network Co-op supports crossplay across all xbox devices and PC. Progression is shared throughout the party so host of game is not the only player progressing.

    Players can now replay missions to relive moments in the story and unlock missed achievements.

    New Achievements

    With the introduction of Co-op campaign, there is now 24 new achievements for campagin. Some can be done solo, but some will require more than oneself.

    Forge Beta

    Forge has finally arrived to Halo Inifinite. The creation tool lauches with six canvas maps, a new visual scripting engine, object scaling, lighting and audio tools, bot support, huge budget increases, file sharing capabilities, and more.

    All content will carry over once forge comes out of beta so no worries of losing any progress.

    New Battle Pass & Events

    This Winter Update bring a whole new battle pass to complete. The pass features 30 tiers of rewards. Among (us?) the rewards, the return of some fan-favoirte cosmetics from Halo Reach.

    Over the coming months, look foward to 2 free events. Each events will bring a further 10 reward tiers.

    In December, Winter Contingency will be declared once more. The event will offer holiday-themed cosemtics.

    In January, the Joint Fire event wil cente raround the JFO-class Mjolnir armor and other related cosmetics.
